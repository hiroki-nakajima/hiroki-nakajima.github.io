Hiroki Nakajima

Hiroki Nakajima (Japanese: 中島 啓貴 Nakajima Hiroki; born in 1992) is a young Japanese mathematician, and an assistant professor of the institute for excellence in higher education at Tohoku University.

He obtained his Ph.D. from Tohoku University in 2019. He study geometry on metric measure spaces. In particular, he is interested in isoperimetric inequalities on metric measure spaces.
